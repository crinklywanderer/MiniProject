# AI Medical Chatbot - PowerPoint Presentation Outline

## Slide 1: Title Slide
- Title: "AI Medical Chatbot"
- Subtitle: "An Intelligent Healthcare Assistant"

## Slide 2: Project Overview
- Purpose: To provide an AI-powered medical chatbot that can answer health-related questions
- Target Users: Patients seeking medical information
- Key Features: Natural language processing, medical knowledge base, user-friendly interface

## Slide 3: Problem Statement
- Challenge: Access to reliable medical information
- Need: Quick and accurate responses to medical queries
- Solution: AI-powered chatbot with medical knowledge

## Slide 4: System Architecture
- Client-Server Architecture
- Frontend: HTML, CSS, JavaScript
- Backend: Flask (Python)
- Components: Chatbot Engine, Controller, Database

## Slide 5: Technical Implementation
- Natural Language Processing using TF-IDF Vectorization
- Cosine Similarity for matching user queries with appropriate responses
- Dataset: Medical Q&A pairs (questions and doctor responses)

## Slide 6: Chatbot Engine
- TF-IDF Vectorization: Converting text to numerical vectors
- Cosine Similarity: Finding the most similar question in the dataset
- Response Threshold: Ensuring quality responses

## Slide 7: Dataset
- Medical Q&A pairs
- Structure: Question titles, detailed questions, doctor responses
- Topics covered: Various medical conditions, symptoms, treatments
